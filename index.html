Researcher (First Year Research Immersion)

My team and I have been researching machine learning and human robot interaction in Mahjong. Our research is detailed in a 10 page research report and a poster that we present at a poster session. The goal of our research is to develop a robotic arm capable of playing Mahjong. We developed a convolutional neural network for image detection using the keras, tensorflow, numpy and glob python libraries. We also used coppelia sim to simulate our robotic arm in a virtual environment. We used answer set programming to implement logic.
